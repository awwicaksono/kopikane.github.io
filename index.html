Type Of Coffee Shop

We offer the best Coffee Shop for you

Coffee Shop

Coffee places that fall into this category provide a comfortable atmosphere for chatting, both indoor, outdoor or both. Customers who come here come not only to enjoy coffee, but also to hang out.

Cafe

Cafe is a place with comfortable vibes, ambience, atmosphere, enviroment and sensation that is better known to hang out or chat while enjoying drinks and food that has been provided

Grab and Go

This type prioritizes the sale of coffee is for customers to take home. Therefore, some places do not provide comfortable seating/tables for customers to enjoy their orders on location.
